About Rusk Media

Rusk Media is a full-stack, GenZ-first digital entertainment company headquartered in Mumbai, India. It specialises in premium content creation through its content studio, operating social and OTT video intellectual properties, and running a social user-generated content (UGC) gaming platform. The company aims to entertain over 100 million people globally. In 2026, Yash Raj Films (YRF) made a strategic investment in Rusk Media to accelerate the vertical entertainment economy in India.
